Privacy Policy

Your data powers Concize, so we explain exactly how it is collected, used, and protected across our marketing workspace.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us (account, workspace, billing, and communication data), content created inside Concize, and limited usage data generated by device, browser, and cookie signals. We do not sell personal data.

1.1 Customer Content

Tasks, messages, attachments, social content drafts, analytics dashboards, and any other material you upload are treated as confidential Customer Content. You control this data and may export or delete it at any time.

1.2 Service Metadata

We process telemetry such as feature adoption, automation success rates, and performance metrics to maintain uptime and shape our roadmap.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide and personalize the Concize experience for each workspace.
  • Secure the platform, detect abuse, and investigate incidents.
  • Deliver support, product updates, onboarding guidance, and billing notices.
  • Build anonymized insights that improve scheduling, forecasting, and OKR planning.

3. Sharing & Subprocessors

We share data only with vetted subprocessors who provide hosting, analytics, communications, or payment services. Each vendor signs data protection terms that mirror our own obligations. We do not allow third parties to use your data for their marketing.

4. International Transfers

Concize uses globally distributed infrastructure with data centers primarily located in the EU and US. Transfers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and regional controls to keep Customer Content local whenever possible.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may request access, correction, export, or deletion of personal data. Submit requests through privacy@concize.com and we will respond within 30 days.

6. Retention

Data tied to an active account is stored for the life of the subscription. After cancellation we retain backups for 30 days unless you request accelerated deletion.

7. Updates

We will post any privacy changes on this page and notify workspace admins by email when updates are material. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
